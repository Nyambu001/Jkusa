Welcome to JKUSA Election Website

The Jomo Kenyatta University Students Association (JKUSA) serves as the official student governing body at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). Established to represent and advocate for the interests of the student community.

About Us

JKUSA Elections

Elections are a fundamental aspect of JKUSA's operations, ensuring democratic representation within the student body. The electoral process is structured to promote fairness and active student participation.Currently, there exists one unified Student Government with branches in the various campuses of the University: Every bona de student of the university is a member of the JKUSA by virtue of their enrolment in the university

Read More

Election Process

The elections are conducted in phases, beginning with the selection of delegates and congress representatives from each department. These representatives then participate in electing the main student council, ensuring a comprehensive representation of the student populace

Electoral Commission

To oversee the elections, an Electoral Commission was established. Notably, there is an extension of the application deadline for commission positions to encourage broader student involvement.

Student Elections

The office of the Dean of Students coordinates and oversees the elections for the Student Governing body.
